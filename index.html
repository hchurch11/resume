About My Career Shift

I am a trained orchestral percussionist and teacher, and the Covid-19 pandemic has left me without a job. Over the past few years, I have entertained the idea of getting into the tech industry. With my music program being cut, and the prospects of playing live music soon are low, I've decided to go forward with a career in tech. I hope to use the skills I have learned as a musician and teacher in my new career. I believe my creative and analytical brain, ability to learn concepts quickly, and my hard work ethic will allow me success as a Full-Stack engineer!
